Title: Customer Support Channels
Category: Customer Service

We offer multiple support channels:

Customer Hotline:
- Available Monday to Friday, 8:00 - 20:00
- Saturday: 9:00 - 17:00
- Services: General inquiries, complaints, service appointments

Online Support:
- Live Chat on our website during business hours
- Email contact form with 24-48 hour response time
- FAQ section for self-service
- Mobile app: In-app support and service booking

Roadside Assistance:
- Available 24/7, 365 days a year
- Included for all vehicles within warranty period
- Services: On-site repair, towing, mobility services
- Can be activated via phone, mobile app, or SOS button in vehicle

Complaint Process:
1. Contact customer hotline or dealer
2. Case number assigned for tracking
3. Regional customer relations manager involved if needed
4. Ombudsman process available for unresolved disputes
