Title: Warranty Coverage
Category: Warranty & Guarantees

We provide comprehensive warranty coverage for all new vehicles:

Standard Warranty:
- Duration: 2 years unlimited mileage
- Coverage: All manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
- Exclusions: Normal wear items (brake pads, tires, wiper blades, bulbs)

Paintwork Warranty:
- Duration: 3 years
- Covers paint defects from the manufacturing process
- Does not cover stone chips, scratches, or environmental damage

Corrosion Warranty:
- Duration: 12 years
- Covers perforation corrosion (rust-through from inside out)
- Requires regular inspection at authorized service partners

Electric Vehicle Battery Warranty:
- Duration: 8 years or 160,000 km (whichever comes first)
- Covers battery capacity below 70% of original
- Covers battery cell defects and high-voltage components

Extended Warranty:
- Available for purchase before standard warranty expires
- Options: 1 or 2 additional years
- Can be purchased at any authorized dealer

All warranty claims must be processed through authorized service partners.
