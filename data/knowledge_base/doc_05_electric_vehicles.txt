Title: Electric Vehicles & Charging
Category: Electric Mobility

Our electric vehicle lineup includes fully electric (BEV) and plug-in hybrid (PHEV) models with ranges from 400 to 630 km (WLTP).

Charging Options:
- Home Charging: Wallbox (11 kW or 22 kW AC)
- Public Charging: Access to 600,000+ charging points across Europe
- DC Fast Charging: Up to 200 kW depending on model
- Charging time (10-80%): Approximately 30-35 minutes at fast chargers

Charging Tariffs:
- Pay-per-use with competitive per-kWh rates
- Monthly subscription with reduced rates available
- Partnerships with major charging networks for preferential pricing

Battery Technology:
- Latest generation electric drive technology
- Battery pre-conditioning for optimal fast charging
- Recyclable battery design with second-life applications

Government Incentives:
- Environmental bonus varies by country
- Tax benefits for electric company cars
- Reduced parking fees and toll exemptions in select cities
