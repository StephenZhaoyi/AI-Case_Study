Title: Vehicle Features & Configuration Options
Category: Product Information

Our vehicles offer extensive configuration options through the Online Configurator:

Exterior:
- Over 15 standard paint colors plus premium and matte finish options
- Multiple alloy wheel designs ranging from 17" to 21" depending on the model
- Exterior trim choices: chrome, gloss black, or satin finishes

Interior:
- Upholstery options: fabric, synthetic leather, genuine leather, and premium leather
- Interior trim: wood, aluminum, and carbon fiber finishes available
- Ambient lighting with configurable colors

Technology Packages:
- Digital cockpit with navigation system
- Head-Up Display
- Parking assistant with surround-view cameras
- Digital car key compatible with smartphones

Customers can save and share their configurations online. A completed configuration can be sent directly to a dealer for a personalized offer. Configuration changes are possible until the vehicle enters production.
