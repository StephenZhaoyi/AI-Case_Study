Title: Ordering Process
Category: Sales

The vehicle ordering process follows these steps:

1. Configuration:
   - Use the Online Configurator on our website or visit a dealer
   - Select model, engine, exterior, interior, and packages
   - Save your configuration with a code

2. Dealer Consultation:
   - Present your configuration to a dealer
   - Discuss pricing, financing, and leasing options
   - Check available inventory vs. factory order

3. Order Placement:
   - Sign the purchase or lease agreement
   - Pay the deposit (typically 10% of vehicle price)
   - Receive order confirmation with estimated delivery date

4. Production & Delivery:
   - Production timeline: typically 6-10 weeks for standard configurations
   - Track production status via the mobile app
   - Choose between dealer delivery or factory pickup experience
   - Vehicle handover includes a feature demonstration

Financing Options:
- Competitive financing rates through our financial services
- Balloon financing with guaranteed future value
- Leasing for private and business customers

Order modifications are possible until the vehicle enters production.
